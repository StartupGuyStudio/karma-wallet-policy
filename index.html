Karma Wallet – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Overview

Karma Wallet ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use the Karma Wallet application.

By accessing or using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and display name
  • Email address
  • Authentication data via Google, email, or other supported login providers

Reflection & Activity Data

  • Daily journal entries
  • Karma scores and tracking metrics
  • AI-generated insights and reports
  • Progress summaries and challenge activity

Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage logs
  • Timezone and region settings

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, biometric data, banking credentials, or payment card numbers.

3. Subscription & Access Model

Karma Wallet offers time-based access in the form of challenge extensions ("days access"). When a user purchases access, the selected number of days is added to their existing journey.

Purchasing additional days does not reset progress or remove stored data. It only extends access to app features, including tracking, reports, and AI-generated insights.

Payment transactions are processed by authorized app marketplace providers (such as Apple App Store or Google Play). We do not directly collect or store payment card details.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized reflections and AI-powered insights
  • To calculate and display progress analytics
  • To manage subscription access and active days
  • To maintain platform security and prevent misuse
  • To improve functionality and user experience

5. AI Processing Disclosure

Journal entries may be processed using AI systems to generate reflections, summaries, and analytics. These systems operate under secure processing standards.

We do not sell or publicly share your journal content.

6. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security practices including encrypted data transmission, controlled access systems, and secure authentication mechanisms.

Although no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to protect user information.

Account Deletion & Journey Reset

Users may request deletion of their account at any time directly from the Profile section within the app.

Selecting the “Reset Journey” or “Delete Account” option will permanently remove all associated data, including journal entries, karma scores, streaks, analytics, reports, and historical activity.

This action is irreversible. Once completed, your data cannot be restored.

Deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
